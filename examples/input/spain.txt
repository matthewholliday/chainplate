Spain is a country located in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula. It is known for its rich history, diverse cultures, and vibrant traditions. Spain boasts a variety of landscapes, from the sunny Mediterranean coastlines and beautiful beaches to the mountainous regions of the Pyrenees and Sierra Nevada. The country is famous for its art, architecture, and cuisine, including dishes like paella and tapas. Major cities such as Madrid, Barcelona, and Seville are renowned for their historical landmarks, lively festivals, and unique blend of modern and traditional influences. Spain is also recognized for its contributions to literature, music, and sports, making it a popular destination for travelers from around the world.
